# SmartHire – HR Management System

## Overview

This project was developed as part of the PIDEV – 3rd Year Engineering Program at Esprit School of Engineering (Academic Year 2025–2026).

SmartHire is a Human Resources Management System designed to digitalize and optimize HR operations within an organization.

The application integrates recruitment, training, events, and leave management into one unified system.

## Features

### Recruitment Management
- Job offer management
- Candidate application tracking
- Interview scheduling
- Recruitment workflow monitoring

### Training Management
- Training program creation
- Employee training registration
- Training tracking and evaluation

### Event Management
- Event creation and planning
- Participant management
- Event scheduling

### Leave Management
- Leave request submission
- Leave approval workflow
- Leave history tracking
